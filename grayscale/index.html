About Me

Hello, I am an undergraduate student from IIT Kharagpur in the third year of Electrical Engineering enrolled in its Dual Degree Course. This is my home page and do feel free to browse the tabs for further details about me. Originally, I am from Mumbai, and have done majority of my schooling there.

I hail from a middle class Tamilian background and have travelled around India to quite a few places, be it Puducherry, Bangalore, or Pune, or be it even stretching past the borders of India into Nepal. Likewise, I like meeting people of different backgrounds and exchanging cultures and ideas. Moreover, I like visiting places in the view of tourism and scenic beauty. Music is also something which I appreciate.

I also play the tabla , an Indian Hindustani classical instrument based on percussion and rhythm. I also like listening to classical music and western instrumentals. I especially prefer the works of Ilayaraja, A.R. Rahman and Shankar Mahadevan when it comes to their contributions in this regard. Apart from music, I also take interest in sports, with due bias towards cricket, tennis and table tennis. I admire Sachin Tendulkar and Rafael Nadal in their respective sports. I enjoy playing table tennis during my leisure time.
